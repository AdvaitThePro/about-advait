Taxation

In Singapore, companies are required to file their corporate tax returns with the Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS). Tax preparation and filing is a responsibility of all business owners who have to ensure that their corporate tax matters are filed before the given deadline to avoid civil and criminal penalties. However, the nature of tax returns preparation can be seen as complex and time-consuming. In some cases, it can be a distraction to those business owners whose focus is still primarily on managing the business operation. Our team of tax professionals are competent and possess the in-depth technical knowledge to assist businesses across a wide spectrum of tax services. We pride ourselves to be able to deliver a seamless tax planning, advisory and solution that focuses on helping business leaders to make informed tax decisions, and at the same time relieving them of the tedium in tax compliance.

Besides statutory tax filing, we also assist our valued clients in attending to queries from IRAS, apply tax ruling and resolving tax issues.
• Annual Tax filing procedures for Companies incorporated in Singapore
• Personal Tax Filing
• GST Quarterly Returns
• Withholding Tax
• Tax Computation and Filing of Estimated Chargeable Income.
• Resolving tax disputes with a view to minimizing tax and penalty exposure
