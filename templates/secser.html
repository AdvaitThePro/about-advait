Secretarial Services

Kaizen Management Consultancy specializes in assisting clients to set up the most appropriate corporate vehicle for a presence in Singapore.

• Establishment of a Singapore Company (Resident/Non- Resident)
• Foreign Corporations- Registration of a Singapore Branch/ Setting up a Singapore Representative office.
• Provision of Registered office facilities
• Nominee secretary and Professional Nominee director services

• Statutory Filings with the Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority
• De-Registration services including assistance on Winding-Up and Striking Off.
• Assisting in preparation of commercial agreements
• Assistance in maintaining company ESOP.
